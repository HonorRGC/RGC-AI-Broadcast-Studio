# RGC AI Broadcast Studio - Future Ideas

## Broadcast Intelligence

The Race Director should decide:

- What deserves airtime
- When to interrupt commentary
- When to remain quiet
- When to trigger replay
- When to switch cameras
- When to perform a field rundown
- When to discuss strategy
- When to introduce drivers
- When to summarize the race

## Caution Broadcast Flow

- Yellow flag comes out
- Announce caution immediately
- Identify possible cause of caution
- Show replay of incident in real time
- Show replay again in slow motion
- Discuss damage, fault, and affected drivers
- Wait until field is doubled up / one lap to green
- Run down the field from leader to last
- Mention biggest movers and drivers who lost positions
- Announce green flag restart

## Pre-Race and Restart Coverage

- During pacing before the original green flag, introduce the full starting lineup
- Give driver name, starting position, car number, vehicle, iRating, country, and short driver note
- Give a short track description before the start
- Mention weather, air temp, track temp, and race length
- Announce every green flag restart
- Announce when the yellow flag comes out
- During cautions, pause normal pass calls
- Use caution time for field rundown, replay review, sponsor reads, and strategy talk

## Incident Replay System

- Detect off-track incidents
- Detect wall contact
- Detect car-to-car contact
- Detect 2x, 4x, and higher incident events
- Show important incidents even if they do not cause a caution
- Trigger replay review for major incidents
- Let Mike call the replay
- Let Jeff explain fault, damage, and impact
- Track repeat incidents for the same driver
- Avoid showing minor incidents unless they affect the race

## Caution Replay System

- Detect the incident that caused a caution
- Automatically jump replay back 10–20 seconds before the incident
- Show the replay in real time
- Show the replay again in slow motion
- Let the commentator explain what happened during the replay
- Return to live coverage before the restart
- Use replay time to fill caution periods naturally
- Combine replay coverage with a field rundown before going green

## Caution / Restart Coverage

- Detect caution periods
- Stop normal pass commentary during cautions
- Give a field rundown under yellow
- Highlight biggest movers
- Highlight drivers who lost positions
- Discuss pit strategy
- Build restart storylines
- Countdown to green flag
